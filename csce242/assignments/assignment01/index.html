Electric vehicles are the future of transportation

Electric cars have been growing in popularity over the past decade, and for good reason. Electric cars are significantly more efficient than traditional gas cars in terms of CO2 emmisions, even when taking into account the emmisions required to make the car. Furthermore, electric vehicles can be charged using energy produced from renewable energy such as solar or wind. This means that an electric car can be powered by the sun.

Because of the rise in popularity of electric cars, many car companies have developed electric car models. As a result, the cost of electric cars has decreased significantly. What makes EVs expensive is the cost of the battery packs used and the slow charging time. Battery technology is rapidly improving to meet the demand, and each year electric cars are getting better and better.

Why electric vehicles are great:

  • Less CO2 emissions
  • Can be combined with renewable energy for carbon neutral transportation
  • Overall more efficient
